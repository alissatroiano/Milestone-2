How To Play

What is it?
Game Of Facts is a trivia game about the real-life history that inspired George R.R. Martin's popular book and TV series, 'Game Of Thrones'.
How do I play?
Just read each question, click on the right answer (if you know it) and read some new interesting facts. Check out your score when you're done and play again! Enjoy!
